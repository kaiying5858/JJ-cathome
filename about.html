Our Story

Cat Nest Home began with a simple observation: cats spend up to 16 hours a day sleeping, yet many cat beds available in the market weren't designed with cats' natural behaviors and preferences in mind.

Founded by a team of cat lovers and design enthusiasts, we set out to create cat beds that would not only provide ultimate comfort for our feline friends but also complement modern home dÃ©cor. Our designs are inspired by extensive research into cat behavior and sleep patterns, combined with input from veterinarians and cat behaviorists.

Today, we're proud to offer a wide range of cat beds that cater to different cat personalities, sizes, and living environments. From cozy enclosed spaces for shy cats to elevated perches for adventurous climbers, each product is thoughtfully designed to enhance your cat's quality of life.
